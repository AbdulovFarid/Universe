Privacy Policy for Vanguard Video Bot

1. Information We Collect
Our application interacts with TikTok API to provide automated video management services. We collect:

Public Profile Information: Username and display name to personalize your dashboard.

Authentication Tokens: Access tokens provided by TikTok via OAuth to perform actions on your behalf.

Video Metadata: Titles and descriptions of videos you choose to upload through our service.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data strictly for the following purposes:

To facilitate video uploads from our processing engine to your TikTok account.

To maintain your session and connection to TikTok API.

Note: We do not sell, rent, or share your TikTok data with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Authentication tokens are stored in an encrypted database.

Processed videos are deleted from our servers immediately after a successful upload to TikTok.

4. User Control and Data Deletion
You can revoke our access at any time via:

TikTok Security Settings (Manage App Access).

Sending a formal deletion request to: tripakwotgmail.com
Upon request, we will delete all your associated data from our database within 24 hours.
